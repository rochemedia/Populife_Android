What unlocking methods does the smart lock support?


Here are some of the main ways to unlock:
1. APP unlocking
   It refers to unlocking with the Bluetooth key through the Bluetooth of the mobile phone. The Bluetooth key can only be sent to the registered accounts of the APP, and can be set to the specific validity period or frozen or deleted.
2. Password unlocking
   It refers to unlocking with the password through the numeric keypad of lock. The password is dynamically generated based on the password type and validity period. The supported types are permanent, period, one-time and cyclic.
  • Permanent passwords do not expire and can be used permanently;
  • The period password can specify the start and end time, up to 3 years;
  • The one-time password will expire once it is used;
  • The cyclic password is available on the specified date and time period, such as from 10 am to 11 am every Monday.
